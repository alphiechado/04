H1 2022 (Q1 & Q2) appraisal commences

Dear Colleagues,
As you are aware, the H1 2022 (Q1 & Q2) assessment is still ongoing and is slated to conclude on July 25, 2022. Please be informed that participation in appraisals is mandatory for current employees, and whether you complete this appraisal or not, it will be factored into your final annual performance score. This clearly indicates that you will earn a score of 0% if your appraisal is not completed by the due day. New/recent hires awaiting confirmation are exempt from this evaluation. I have observed some new hires using the portal to take an appraisal. In order to participate in these appraisals, you must be eligible. If you have any questions about this eligibility or your confirmation status, please contact HR. We kindly request that managers take note of the recommendations made in the appraisal report from the previous year. As you complete your appraisals, please make sure to check the following;
1. Use the comment section during self-evaluation to provide more details about your performance. Please provide a logical, succinct and detailed explanation of how the KPI performance was assessed and how the score was determined. Your justification lays the groundwork for and substantially contributes to the score by demonstrating that the KPI has been measured logically.
2. Documentary or digital data proof should be attached to substantiate the score. This demonstrates that the results are accurate and that a data source from which the data was derived is present. It also gives the ARC a foundation to evaluate your performance during calibration.
3. Include KPIs that are relevant to your job role. The quality of KPIs is more important than their quantity. Keep your deliverables in mind when adding KPIs, and avoid including pointless KPIs that have nothing to do with your deliverables.
4. Add KPIs that are commensurate to your grade level.
5. Give your primary KPIs more weight (KPIs that are critical to your job role). 6. Make sure you give the appropriate KPI type to the appropriate KPI by reading about the different KPI types (please read PDF attached). When calibrating KPIs, those with incorrect KPI ratings as a result of choosing the incorrect KPI type will receive a score of 0.
7. Avoid verbatim copying of your division's KPIs and scores from the divisional goal sheet.
8. Individual EDP Compliance KPI target should not exceed 10%.
Supervisors should kindly encourage adherence to the aforementioned rules. Please check the computation logic for each KPI during appraisal before granting/approving any score. When appraisals are completed poorly, supervisors will be held accountable.
If you have any questions or concerns or need any support, please email perfmgt@ipnxnigeria.net or mnjoku@ipnxnigeria.net
Regards.
