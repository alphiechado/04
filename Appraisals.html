Update on Appraisals

Dear All,
Trust this mail meets you well.
Apologies for the late update on Q1 2022 appraisals. Appraisals are expected to be carried out every quarter (for employees on Assistant Manager grade level and above) and Half-yearly (for Officers and Senior Officers). However, at this time, we deem it best to put Q1 2022 on hold. This is mainly to tighten the divisional and departmental SLAs. We have the approval of Management to combine the Q1 and Q2 appraisal for this year. Hence, for 2022 appraisal cycle, we will have the following 3 appraisal periods;
1. H1 (Q1&Q2) 2022 Appraisal (for all confirmed staff, all grade levels) in July
2. Q3 2022 Appraisal (for Assistant Managers & above only) in October
3. Q4/H2 Appraisals (for all confirmed staff) in January

Please remember that participation in appraisals is compulsory. Your total appraisal score for 2022 appraisal will be calculated by taking the average of the number of appraisals expected of your grade level, for example, an AM's total appraisal score will be the average of 3 appraisals. So, failure to complete any appraisal will mean a 0 score, and the average of 3 will still be used in computing your total score. H1 2022 appraisal will commence in the 1st week of July. You are expected to combine/sum Q1 and Q2 KPIs targets when setting your KPIs on SeamlessHR. Please do not add separate KPIs for Q1 and Q2. H1 (Q1&Q2) Appraisal Period is enabled on SeamlessHR to enable you to add your KPIs/Objectives on the appraisal portal. Ensure you set your KPIs on time to avoid unnecessary pressure/rush during appraisals. I will be sending email reminders via email. Meanwhile, lets' ensure we continue to work with our goal sheets and apply PEM into our work.
Please send a mail to or if you have any questions.

Thank you.

Performance Management
