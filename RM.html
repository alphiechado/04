Final Extension - Q4/H2 2021 Appraisal

Dear All,
Further to our mail below, we understand that the alteration to the Objectives/KPI setting workflow impacted some people and delayed their appraisals. Although the issue has long been fixed, we acknowledge your disappointment and once again apologize for the inconvenience. We had reached out to those who were affected and we want to assure everyone of our continued commitment to improve the overall appraisal experience for both appraiser and appraisee. Consequently, we have the approval of Management to extend Q4/H2 2021 appraisal by 1 week. This is especially to enable those impacted complete their appraisal.

There will be no sanctions (appraisal marks deducted for failing to submit appraisal by the 30th as earlier communicated). However there will be no further extension!!. We encourage everyone to maximize these 7 days. The final deadline for submission of Q4/H2 2021 appraisal is now 11:59PM, Sunday, February 6, 2022. Once again, we advise against leaving Objectives/KPI setting and appraisals till the last minute, as it only gives room to unnecessary pressure, back and forth and eventual errors. If you have any questions, please send an email to mnjoku@ipnxnigeria.net or perfmgt@ipnxnigeria.net.
Thank you.
