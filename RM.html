Q4/H2 2021 Appraisal Commences

Dear Colleagues,
Compliments of the season and a Happy New Year!!.
I am pleased to welcome you to 2022!. As you are aware, the beginning of January marks the kick off of our year end appraisals, hence this is to formally inform you of the commencement of Q4/H2 2021 performance appraisal. This appraisal covers October to December 2021 for Assistant Managers and above and July to December 2021 for Officers and Senior Officers, and is applicable to all employees who are eligible for an appraisal at this time. You can now access the appraisal portal https://ipnx.seamlesshrms.com to begin your self-assessment. I have attached some PDF to guide you on how to use the appraisal interface, it contains a description of KPI Types, Screenshots of steps to follow to complete an appraisal and some useful tips. The deadline for this appraisal period is January 30th 2022. All access to the appraisal portal will close on the end date. Please ensure to follow up with your supervisor to complete your appraisal within the given time frame. If you have any questions, please write to
